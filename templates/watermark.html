Image Watermarking

Accepted formats: PNG, JPEG, DICOM (converted)
This text will be embedded in the image as a watermark

Digital watermarking embeds authentication markers in medical images to ensure their integrity and prevent unauthorized modifications.

Benefits of Watermarking
Image Authentication

Verify image source and integrity

Tamper Detection

Identify unauthorized modifications

Copyright Protection

Prevent unauthorized use of medical imagery

Use Cases
  • Radiology Images: Add patient ID and acquisition date
  • Research Data: Mark with institution name and study ID
  • Telemedicine: Add physician identifiers and timestamp
  • Medical Records: Embed healthcare provider information
Watermarks can be visible or invisible depending on security requirements.
