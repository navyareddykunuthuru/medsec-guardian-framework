Why Verify Images?
  • Prevent Fraud: Detect unauthorized modifications to medical images
  • Ensure Integrity: Confirm images haven't been altered since creation
  • Legal Protection: Verify authenticity for legal or compliance purposes
  • Patient Safety: Prevent diagnosis based on tampered imagery
Regular verification of medical images helps maintain data integrity in healthcare systems.
